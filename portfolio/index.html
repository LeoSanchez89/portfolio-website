A litte more about me.

I'm from New York - the city that never sleeps! I'm very much a people person, and I have an inquisitive mind - which led me to study psychology at school. Ever since I can remember, I've had a thirst for knowledge and a desire to understand how things work. As a youngster, I would always take things apart to study or modify them, and attempt to fix broken items. I'm a tinkerer, critical thinker, and I always ask why.
